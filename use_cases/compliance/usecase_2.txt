### Notes to the Interim Financial Statements

1.  **Basis of Preparation:** These condensed interim financial statements have been prepared in accordance with the accounting standards issued by the Accounting and Auditing Organisation for Islamic Financial Institutions (AAOIFI).

2.  **Murabaha and Commodity Murabaha Receivables:** Represent amounts due from customers under Murabaha and Commodity Murabaha financing arrangements. These are stated net of allowances for doubtful debts.

3.  **Ijara Assets:** Consist primarily of properties leased to customers under operating lease agreements. These are stated at cost less accumulated depreciation. Depreciation is calculated on a straight-line basis over the estimated useful lives of the assets.

4.  **Istisna' Assets:** Represent the cost of construction projects in progress under Istisna' contracts.

5.  **Sukuk Investments:** Represent investments in Shari'ah-compliant Sukuk.

6.  **Investment Accounts:** Represent amounts held on behalf of investment account holders. Profit is distributed to account holders based on performance.

7.  **Shariah Supervisory Board:** The bank's operations are supervised by an independent Shariah Supervisory Board to ensure compliance with Islamic principles.
