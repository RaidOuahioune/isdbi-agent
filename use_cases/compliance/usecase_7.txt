**Notes to the Condensed Interim Financial Statements:**

1.  **Basis of Preparation:** These condensed interim financial statements have been prepared in accordance with the accounting standards issued by [Country's Regulatory Body], and based on historical cost convention, except for certain financial assets and liabilities that are measured at fair value.

2.  **Murabaha Receivables:** Represents amounts due from customers for Murabaha transactions.

3.  **Istisna’ Assets:** Represents the cost of assets under construction for Istisna’ contracts.

4.  **Ijarah Assets:** Represents assets leased to customers under Ijarah agreements. These are accounted for as operating leases.

5.  **Sukuk Investments:** Represents investments in Sharia-compliant Sukuk.

6.  **Investment Accounts:** These accounts represent funds received from customers for investment purposes. Profit is distributed to investment account holders based on performance.

7.  **Shari’ah Supervisory Board:** The Bank operates under the guidance of a dedicated Shari’ah Supervisory Board, ensuring all activities comply with Islamic principles.

8. Figures may not add up due to rounding.

---

VIOLATIONS:

*   **FAS 7:** The profit-sharing ratio (or other basis) for investment accounts is not specified in Note 6 or elsewhere.
*   **FAS 32:** Ijarah assets are incorrectly classified and accounted for as operating leases in Note 4, when they should be classified as finance leases.
