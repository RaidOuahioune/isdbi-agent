**Notes to the Condensed Interim Financial Statements**

1.  **Basis of Preparation:** These condensed interim financial statements have been prepared in accordance with the accounting standards issued by the Accounting and Auditing Organization for Islamic Financial Institutions (AAOIFI) and applicable local regulations.

2.  **Significant Accounting Policies:** The accounting policies adopted are consistent with those of the annual financial statements for the year ended December 31, 2023.

3.  **Ijarah Assets:** Ijarah assets primarily consist of properties leased to customers under operating lease agreements.

4.  **Financing Activities:** Financing activities include Murabaha, Ijarah, and Istisna' transactions.

5.  **Sukuk Investments:** These represent investments in Shari'ah-compliant Sukuk certificates.

6.  **Shari'ah Supervisory Board:** The Bank operates under the guidance of a Shari'ah Supervisory Board, ensuring compliance with Islamic principles.

---

VIOLATIONS:

*   **FAS 7:**  Omission of profit-sharing ratio or detailed allocation method between owners and investment account holders in the Statement of Income or related notes.
*   **FAS 32:** Misreporting of Ijarah assets as operating leases in Note 3, when in reality the bank is using finance leases, thus incorrectly classifying the assets.
